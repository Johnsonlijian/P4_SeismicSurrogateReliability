## Manuscript safety boundary

No unverified literature item was converted into a final BibTeX entry. Literature-dependent novelty claims were kept bounded: the paper claims a reliability-oriented diagnostic for finite-label event-separated surrogate screening, not a universal conformal theorem, a new PBEE loss model, or a calibrated design-code reliability index.
